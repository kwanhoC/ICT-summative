What is Global Warming

When we ask "What important problem on Earth?" We probably think of Global Warming, Ice melting in North Pole, unusual disaster happen in
different countries. Global warming has been one of the serious problem that happening on Earth. So let's start with the basics, what is Global
Warming? When you are driving your car, or a factory are burning stuff. They all release Carbon Dioxide (co2). This carbon Dioxide and
other greenhouse gases trap inside Earth's atmosphere, and can't escape from it, the planet's temperature rises. That's called global
warming, which causes climate change.
